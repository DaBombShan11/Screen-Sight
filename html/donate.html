Why We Need Your Support

Seven students from Bowie State University collaborated to develop Screen Sight, a software application designed to enhance the accessibility of personal devices for millions of users worldwide. Screen Sight recently won $20,000 in a NASA pitch competition, highlighting both the innovation behind the product and the demand for its capabilities.

To bring Screen Sight to market within the next two years, we seek donations of any amount to support our research and development efforts. Our prototype impressed the judges, and with your support, we can turn our vision into reality!
